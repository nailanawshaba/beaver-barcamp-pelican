Welcome to Beaver BarCamp 12

BarCamp is an ad-hoc gathering born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment. It is an intense event with discussions, demos and interaction from attendees.

What kind of sessions happen at Beaver BarCamp? All kinds! While most may be tech oriented, we encourage any kind of PG-13 session. You can see a list of previous Beaver BarCamp sessions to get an idea of what you may find.
